Citing pyWitness

Any publications in which pyWitness was used should cite our preprint:

  • Mickes, L., Seale-Carlisle, T. M., Chen, X., & Boogert, S. (2022, March 9). pyWitness 1.0: A Python eyewitness identification analysis toolkit. doi.org/10.31234/osf.io/5ruks.

Citations show the code usage, allowing us to continue to develop and maintain pyWitness.

Contacting us

We welcome your constructive thoughts on pyWitness. To report issues, ask questions, make comments, and provide suggestions, go to the issue tracker and click on the green “New issue” button.
